About Us

Indian Dental Association (IDA) is an independent and recognized voice of dental professionals in India. IDA represents more than 75,000 dental professionals and has 35 state branches and 450 local branches. IDA MALABAR branch, is one of the largest branches in the country, and we are committed to improving public oral health by promoting advanced, science-based dentistry that puts the patient at the centre. We acknowledge that oral health is an integral part of general health and well-being. Through various initiatives and campaigns, IDA Malabar branch reaches out to the public to safeguard and promote optimal oral healthcare. Our latest initiative is a first of its kind website called as IDA MALABAR CARE.

Mission

IDA's mission is optimal oral health for all. With this mission in mind, we have come up with a new project targeting differently abled individuals, especially those who are wheel chair bound and have limited access to dental care. IDA MALABAR CARE- Connect with us And Restore your dentition is, Is focused to rehabilitate the dentition of such individuals.

Vision

The association's vision is to improve oral health and quality of life and achieve optimal oral health for all. We also aim to represent the dental profession and support members in the provision of comprehensive and quality oral health care.

Motto - IDA MALABAR CARE

IDA MALABAR CARE-Connect with us And Restore your dentition is the brain child of our branch focused to deliver dental care to physically challenged individuals. Through this venture, we aim to provide the link to the wheel chair friendly dental clinics of our members located in CALICUT. Those clinics in Calicut which are in ground floor,having ramp or lift facility, that are amenable for wheelchair locomotion are registered in this web page. A concerned individual can access this web page, check out the wheel chair friendly clinics that are in their vicinity, book an appointment and get quality treatment done through our members.
